FAQs

What is VApp?

VApp is a platform designed with the goal to help you improve your digital literacy and keep you safe online. It started out as an idea for a capstone project by a group of students at McMaster University.

How does VApp work?

When you sign up and create an account on VApp, you will be able to add one of your social media accounts. We will analyze (but NOT store) some privacy settings from that social media account then inform you about the implications of those settings.

Which social medias do you support?

We currently support Facebook, Instagram and Twitter.
